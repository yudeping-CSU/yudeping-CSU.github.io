About

Academic Profile

I am a lecturer at the School of Business, Central South University. I received my Ph.D. in Management Science and Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2024.

My work studies how transport policies, infrastructure investment, and spatial economic mechanisms shape travel behavior, urban structure, and policy equity.
